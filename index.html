HERO CITY REJECTS

Where even the most unlikely of us can be heroes!


Welcome to Hero City

In a world where nearly every body has been enhanced with special abilities thanks to genetic augmentation, heroes are the pinnacle of society. But with so many powers running around, the government felt it necessary to impose rigorous certification tests to allow people to use their abilities freely. Pathways are lined out for youth who have abilities. Some are considered more gifted than others. But just because you aren't chosen, doesn't mean you can't still become a hero!

GRAFITTI

Name: Diego Morales

Codename: Graffiti

Age: 17

Ability: Although he disguises it as a form of art manipulation, Diego has the ability to manipulate water. Since he lacks the ability to manifest it spontaneously or manipulate large bodies of it, he creates his own water based aerosol can's and fights using a variety of spray cans. He is also an excellent hand to hand combatant.
